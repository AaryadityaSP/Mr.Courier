Reschedule your package

If due to some unforeseen circumstances, you are unable to receive the parcel on the date of delivery given to you, dont worry! MRCOURIER allowes it's customers to conveniently change the day of expected delivery of their parcel provided the waiting period do not exceed one month. In order to reschedule your parcel, Follow the below steps:

Follow the steps

  • Go to the support section of out website listed at the top

  • Connect to our customer care expert by booking an appointment

  • After the appointment, a confirmation mail will be initiated and by confirming it, your parcel delivery will be rescheduled to your desired date.

  • You can also use the link received at the time the order was pickedup
