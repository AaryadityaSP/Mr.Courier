FAQ's

Q. What is MRCOURIER?
MRCOURIER is a trusted parcel delivery service that specializes in fast, secure, and reliable delivery of your packages and documents to their destination.
Q. How do I use MRCOURIER to send a parcel?
To send a parcel with MRCOURIER, you can create an account on our website, input the recipient's address, select the delivery service, and schedule a pickup or drop-off at one of our convenient locations.
Q. What types of items can I send with MRCOURIER?
You can use MRCOURIER to send a wide range of items, including documents, packages, gifts, and more. Please note that there may be restrictions on certain items, such as hazardous materials.
Q. What are the delivery options available with MRCOURIER?
MRCOURIER offers a variety of delivery options, including standard, express, and same-day delivery, depending on your needs and location.
Q. How can I track my parcel's delivery status?
You can easily track your parcel's delivery status by entering the provided tracking number on our website. Real-time tracking updates are available for your convenience.
Q. Are my parcels insured when using MRCOURIER?
Yes, MRCOURIER provides parcel insurance options to ensure your packages are protected in case of loss or damage during transit. The level of coverage can be selected during the booking process.
Q. What are the delivery times and rates with MRCOURIER?
Delivery times and rates vary depending on the delivery service chosen, the destination, and the size and weight of the parcel. You can find detailed information on our website or by contacting our customer support.
Q. Can I change the delivery schedule after booking?
Yes, you can request changes to the delivery schedule, subject to certain conditions. Please contact our customer support for assistance.
Q. What are the packaging guidelines for parcels sent through MRCOURIER?
We recommend using sturdy and secure packaging to protect your items during transit. Specific packaging guidelines can be found on our website.
Q. What should I do if my parcel is lost or damaged during delivery?
In the unfortunate event of loss or damage, please contact our customer support immediately. We will assist you in filing a claim and resolving the issue.
Q. Is MRCOURIER available internationally?
Yes, MRCOURIER offers international parcel delivery services to a wide range of destinations. You can check the list of supported countries and regions on our website.
Q. How can I get in touch with MRCOURIER's customer support?
You can contact our customer support team through our website, phone, or email. Visit our "Support" page for more details.
